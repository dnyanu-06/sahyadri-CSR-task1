Project: Market mate -Empowering Small and large Enterprises

Project Idea

Marketmates is an innovative mobile and web-based platform designed to bridge the market access gap for small and micro enterprises (SMEs). This platform aims to empower these businesses by providing them with the digital tools and resources they need to reach a wider customer base, increase their visibility, and streamline their operations. This platform connects environmentally-conscious consumers with eco-friendly SMEs and provides waste disposal and recycling services.
